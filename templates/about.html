About HeartPredict

AI-Powered Heart Disease Prediction System

What is HeartPredict?

HeartPredict is a Machine Learning based web application that predicts whether a person is at risk of heart disease. It uses medical parameters and trained ML models to give accurate results with 95% accuracy.

Our system analyzes multiple health factors to provide instant risk assessment and help you take proactive steps for your heart health.

Input Details

Fill these medical parameters for accurate prediction:

Age Your age in years
Gender Male (1) / Female (0)
Chest Pain Type 0, 1, 2, or 3
Blood Pressure Resting BP (mm Hg)
Cholesterol Serum cholesterol (mg/dl)
Fasting Sugar >120 mg/dl (1) or ≤120 (0)
Max Heart Rate Maximum heart rate achieved
Exercise Angina Yes (1) / No (0)

Result Meaning

High Risk

You may have heart disease. Consult a doctor immediately.

No Disease

You are likely healthy. Continue regular checkups.

Our model provides instant prediction with 95% accuracy based on your inputs.
